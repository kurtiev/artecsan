Full Inventory Audit

Warning: if you start a Full Inventory Audit you will be required to count ALL items. Any items left with a zero quantity will be recorded as zero quantity. If you only wish to count certain items please go back and select “Audit Adjustment”.

Note: If any deliveries were received from 12am – now, please DO NOT include those items in your count otherwise your delivery amounts will be doubled. If your count has not been completed by 12am, your inventory will void and you may be required to begin your count again.

We encourage you not to do your inventory audit while the restaurant is actively selling product because it has the ability to cause an incorrect result.
Inventory counted before opening will show immediate accurate results. Inventory counted after closing may not show accurate results until after sales data is pulled, typically the next morning 3am depending on your POS System.

Adjustment Inventory Audit

Warning: You may use this feature to adjust the quantity of specific items without effecting ALL items, however adjustments are typically considered a negative procedure and will count against you on your Performance Score. If you wish to do a full inventory count please go back and select “Full inventory Audit”.

Note: If any deliveries were received from 12am – now, please DO NOT include those items in your count otherwise the delivery amounts will be doubled. If your adjustments have not been saved by 12am, your adjustments will void and you may be required to begin again.

Adjustment made before opening will show immediate accurate results. Adjustments made after closing may not show accurate results until after sales data is pulled, typically the next morning 3am depending upon your POS System.
